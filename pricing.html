What streaming quality do I get?

All plans include professional HD streaming with adaptive bitrate technology. This means viewers automatically receive the best quality for their internet connection - whether they're on fast WiFi, mobile data, or a slower connection.

The platform automatically adjusts quality in real-time to ensure smooth, buffer-free viewing for everyone. Desktop viewers on fast connections typically see HD quality, while mobile viewers get optimized quality for their device and connection speed.

Why can't Starter plans have custom branding?

Custom branding is a professional feature that helps differentiate plans. If branding matters to your business, Professional plan provides great value with all features unlocked.

We support all Shopify plans - Basic, Shopify, Advanced, and Shopify Plus. Integration works seamlessly via the Storefront API.
